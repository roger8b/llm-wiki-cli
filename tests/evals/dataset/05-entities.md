# The Transformer Paper and Its Authors

In 2017 a team of researchers at Google Brain and Google Research published
"Attention Is All You Need", the paper that introduced the Transformer
architecture. Two of its lead authors were Ashish Vaswani and Noam Shazeer, who
argued that attention mechanisms alone — without recurrence or convolution —
were enough to model sequences.

Ashish Vaswani is a machine learning researcher who later co-founded a startup
focused on large language models. Noam Shazeer is a researcher and engineer
known for work on attention and on scaling neural networks, who went on to
co-found Character.AI, a company building conversational AI agents.

Google, the company where the work originated, continued to invest heavily in
Transformer-based models, and the architecture became the foundation of the
modern language-model industry.
